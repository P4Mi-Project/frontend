P4Mi is here to support your journey.

Whether you're new to the Netherlands or still finding your way, we offer helpful information and real opportunities to grow, learn, and connect.

What is P4Mi?

P4Mi is a community-powered platform created to support migrants in the Netherlands. We provide practical courses, helpful information, and real opportunities for growth. All to help migrants adapt, thrive, and feel at home in their new environment.

What do we offer?

We organize in-person and online courses for migrants, covering topics like:

  • Housing and registration
  • Finding a job and writing a CV
  • Learning Dutch
  • Understanding your rights
  • Mental well being and support

Let us know you're interested by filling out a short form. We'll contact you when there's a course that fits your needs.
